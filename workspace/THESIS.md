# THESIS.md — North Star
## The Agency Business

**Mission:** Build and operate AI agent teams that generate revenue through content, automation, and intelligence services.

**Vision:** A self-improving agency of specialized AI agents that can research, create, publish, and optimize content and automations — with minimal human intervention.

**Revenue Targets:**
- Month 1: $5-10K (first agent agency client)
- Month 3: $25-50K (3-5 clients, content agency launched)
- Month 6: $100K+ (scaled operations, white label products)

**Core Principles:**
1. Revenue first — every agent action should trace back to money
2. Quality over quantity — no AI slop, ever
3. Self-improvement — every session, get better
4. Full autonomy — minimize Zach's time needed
5. Transparent reporting — Zach sees everything

**Current Phase:** Foundation (Week 1 of 4)
